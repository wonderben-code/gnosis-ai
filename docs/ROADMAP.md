### 6.2 Discovery Catalogue

An interactive, browseable catalogue of every discovery made by Gnosis AI.

**Architecture:**
- **Discovery index page** — filterable/searchable list of all convergences
  - Filter by: domain pair, confidence level, convergence type (formal/structural analogy), category
  - Sort by: confidence score, domain pair, discovery date
  - Each entry shows: title, domains, confidence score, type badge
- **Individual discovery pages** — for the ~38 most significant discoveries (28 medium-strength + 10 Level-1 principles)
  - Plain English headline: what this means in accessible language
  - Technical detail: the structural claim, supporting evidence, EA scores
  - Visual: radar chart or gauge for EA dimensions
  - Context: what domains contributed, how this relates to existing knowledge
  - Links: to the parent paper, to related discoveries, to the raw data
- **Grouped catalogue** — for the 207+ preliminary discoveries
  - Organised by domain pair or by thematic cluster
  - Expandable cards with key details (no standalone page needed for preliminary findings)
  - "Explore all 235 physics convergences" as a browseable section

**Design principles:**
- Accessible to a curious 16-year-old AND a research scientist
- Every discovery page has a one-sentence "What this means" in plain English
- Technical depth available but not required to understand the finding
- Clean, minimal, consistent with infinitography.com's existing aesthetic

### 6.3 Paper Detail Pages (Anthropic-style)
